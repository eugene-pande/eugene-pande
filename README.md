## Featured Work

### CMIS v2

Redeveloped the Censorship Management Information System from legacy Drupal to Laravel + Livewire for the Office of Censorship.

- Delivered workflows for film classification, publication registration, premises management, invoicing, payments, licensing, certification, and compliance.
- Implemented role-based access control, audit trails, notification workflows, PDF generation, and exportable reports.
- Built renewal reminders, invoice replacement and auto-cancellation flows, approval logic, and multi-channel notifications.

### AI-Assisted Recruitment Automation & HRMIS

Developed an AI-assisted recruitment and HRMIS platform for the Land Commission of Papua New Guinea.

- Covered the full recruitment lifecycle from vacancy management and applications to interviews, selection workflows, offers, probation, and onboarding.
- Implemented SOP-driven workflow progression, approval processes, audit-friendly status tracking, Livewire dashboards, and PDF reports.
- Added AI-assisted profiling, shortlisting support, candidate recommendations, interview question generation, queued email notifications, and digital signatures.

### CMIS v1

Led the architecture, development, and deployment of the original CMIS platform.
